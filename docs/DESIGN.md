## Brand & Style
The design system is built on the narrative of "Sabor que abraça" (Flavor that embraces). It targets urban professionals and families looking for authentic, high-quality Brazilian gastronomy delivered with care. The personality is homely, flavorful, and fresh—balancing the warmth of a home-cooked meal with the efficiency of a modern service.

The visual style is **Modern Rustic**. It combines the clean, high-utility layouts of modern SaaS with tactile, organic elements inspired by Brazilian kitchens.
